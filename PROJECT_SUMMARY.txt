## üîß Contact Form Setup

### Current Status
The form displays a success message locally, but doesn't send emails yet.

### To Enable Email (Choose One)

**Option 1: Formspree (Free, Easiest)**
1. Go to https://formspree.io
2. Create a free account
3. Set up a form, get your Form ID
4. In `contact.html`, change the form to:
```html
<form action="https://formspree.io/f/YOUR_FORM_ID" method="POST">
```

**Option 2: Netlify (Free Hosting)**
1. Deploy site on Netlify (drag & drop)
2. Add `netlify` attribute to form
3. Forms automatically work!

**Option 3: Custom Backend**
Update `assets/js/script.js` to post to your email service
